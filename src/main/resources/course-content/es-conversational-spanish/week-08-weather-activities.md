## This Week's Goals

- Describe weather conditions
- Talk about hobbies and free time
- Make plans and suggestions

## Essential Vocabulary

- **Hace sol** - It's sunny
- **Hace frío** - It's cold
- **Llueve** - It's raining
- **Jugar** - To play
- **Nadar** - To swim
- **¿Qué te gusta hacer?** - What do you like to do?
