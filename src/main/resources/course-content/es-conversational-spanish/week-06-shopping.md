## This Week's Goals

- Shop for clothes and items
- Ask about prices and sizes
- Describe colors and styles

## Vocabulary

- **¿Cuánto cuesta?** - How much does it cost?
- **La tienda** - Store
- **La ropa** - Clothing
- **La camisa** - Shirt
- **Los pantalones** - Pants
- **El vestido** - Dress
- **Rojo** - Red
- **Azul** - Blue
- **Verde** - Green
- **Amarillo** - Yellow
- **Blanco** - White
- **Negro** - Black
- **Grande** - Big/Large
- **Pequeño** - Small
- **Mediano** - Medium
- **Caro** - Expensive
- **Barato** - Cheap

## Practice Patterns

- Ask prices: "¿Cuánto cuesta esta camisa?"
- Describe items: "Quiero una camisa azul"
- Negotiate: "¿Tiene algo más barato?"
