## This Week's Goals

- Form regular preterite verbs
- Talk about what you did yesterday
- Use time expressions with past tense

## Essential Vocabulary

- **Ayer** - Yesterday
- **Anoche** - Last night
- **La semana pasada** - Last week
- **Hablé** - I spoke
- **Comí** - I ate
- **Viví** - I lived
